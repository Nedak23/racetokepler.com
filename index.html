The Road to Kepler

CITIES

Humanity's Last Hope

When the waters rose and the air turned poisonous, humanity rose to the challenge. The 20 most populous cities of Earth lifted themselves into the air. These are the last monuments of human civilization, each hurtling toward a distant promise of renewal.

Each city-ship has different passive and active abilities. These abilities unlock new synergies and strategies, and with fifteen different cities to choose from, no two games will play the same.

TITANS

Mighty Synthetic Beings

Prior to the exodus from Earth, humanity lost all its animals to rampant disease. But their DNA survived. And as our technology evolved into something approaching consciousness, we built colossal guardians in the image of the creatures that had become myth.

Each unique Titan carries with it epic, game-changing abilities. But in order to add them to your deck you'll have to win their favor by demonstrating your crew's worth.

Race To Victory!

Victory comes from being the first to travel 20 systems to reach Kepler, but the journey requires careful resource management and tactical decisions. The Titan bidding system creates meaningful player interaction without direct combat, while city operations and crew abilities offer multiple paths to victory. Will you lead humanity to a bright new future?
